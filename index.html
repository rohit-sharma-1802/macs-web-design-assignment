The Department of Mathematics (today called, the Department of Mathematical and Computational Sciences) started along with the institute(1960). The Department of Mathematical and Computational Sciences, formerly known as Department of Mathematics, is one of the oldest departments of NITK...Read more

Mission

The mission of the Department of Mathematical And Computational Sciences includes the following:

  1. To impart the best quality education in Mathematical Fundamentals to all the Engineering Students of the institute.
  2. To produce high quality professionals to meet the needs of software industry through the PG programs. To engage in R&D work in the frontier areas of Mathematical and Computational Sciences.

Vision

The vision of Department of Mathematical And Computational Sciences (MACS) is to develop and maintain overall academic excellence in the educational and research activities carried out.
